01

Seismic Interpretation

DHI Identification — 2D Section

2D seismic section with direct hydrocarbon indicator annotation — bright spots, flatspots, polarity reversal, and top reservoir pick identified as fluid contact indicators.

3D TR Amplitude Surface

Autotracked horizon amplitude extraction used to identify lateral lithology and fluid variations across the survey area.

Fault-Constrained TR Surface — 2D

Fault-constrained TR surface displayed as a structure contour map, showing elevation time (ms) and major fault trends across the survey.

3D TR Surface & Difference Map

Thickness variation between interpreted horizons displayed in time (ms), with well trajectories, comparing fault-constrained and unconstrained surface interpretations.

3D Faults & Variance Volume

Fault network extracted from seismic variance attribute, displayed in 3D with well positions, used to constrain structural interpretation.

TR Thickness — Flatspot Surfaces

Isochron map showing reservoir thickness variation across fault blocks, derived from fault-constrained TR surface and flatspot surface picks for east and central blocks.

Wireline Log Analysis

Petrophysical Interpretation

Full petrophysical interpretation including GR cutoff, neutron-density crossplot fluid identification, reservoir zonation, and net-to-gross calculation with fluid contacts (GDT, ODT, WUT).

Multi-Tool Log Display

Lithology and fluid zone picks (gas, oil, brine) across a multi-tool wireline suite, with caliper washout identification indicating poor hole conditions.
